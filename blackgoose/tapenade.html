Tapenade (Olive Spread)

This is a really simple dish to prepare and it's always a big hit at parties. My father recommends:

"Make this the night before so that flowers have time to blend. Just bring it up to room temperature before you serve it. In the winter, try serving it warm."

Ingredients

  • 1 8oz. jar sundreid tomatoes
  • 2 large garlic cloves
  • 2/3 c. kalamata olives
  • 1 t. capers

Instructions

  1. Combine tomatoes & garlic in a food processor. Blend until as smooth as possible
  2. Add capers & olives. Pulse the motor few times until they are incoperated, but still retain some texture
  3. Serve on thin toast rounds with goat cheese & fresh basil garnish (optional)
